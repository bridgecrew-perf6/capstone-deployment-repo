Telecom Demographics Prediction

  You are required to build a model that will predict the demographics of certain users, i.e., their age and gender. For building this model, you have been provided with the data on the users' app downloads and user behavior.

This data will give you information on the details of the mobile phone owned by the users, the activities performed by the users, their GPS coordinates, etc. Now, your aim is to predict the age and the gender of a particular user using this data.
